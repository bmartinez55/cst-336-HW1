What is a Resident Virus?

A Resident virus is one of the two types of viruses that corrupts files.

Unlike the Direct Action Virus, this virus will install themselves onto the computer.

Some experts acknowledge this viruses to be more dangerous than Direct Action Viruses.

These viruses can be catergorized into two types, fast infectors and slow infectors.

Fast infector's only objective to cause as much damage as possible and are easily identifiable.

Slow infector's symptoms develop much slower which causes them to be hard to detect.
