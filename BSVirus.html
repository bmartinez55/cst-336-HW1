What is a Boot Sector Virus?

A Boot Sector Virus is one of the most well known and dangerous.

This virus infects the master root and are incredibly difficult to eliminate.

Most of the time, it will end up in installing a new Operating System or a full system format.

These viruses are typically stored on external hard drives.

Over the years computer BIO's have improved to reduce the risk of computers getting infected.
